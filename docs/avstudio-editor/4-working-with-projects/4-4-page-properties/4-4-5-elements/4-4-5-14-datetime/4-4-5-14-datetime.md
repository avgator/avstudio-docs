# DateTime Widget

The `dateTime` widget displays date and time information in your AVStudio interface.

## Overview

_Documentation coming soon._

This widget provides date and time display functionality.

## How to Add a DateTime Widget

1. Open your project in AVStudio and go to the `Editor` view.
2. From the elements panel under Widgets, select `dateTime`.
3. The dateTime widget will appear in your layout.
