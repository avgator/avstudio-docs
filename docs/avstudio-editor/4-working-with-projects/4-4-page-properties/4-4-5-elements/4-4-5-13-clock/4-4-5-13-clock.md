# Clock Widget

The `clock` widget displays time information in your AVStudio interface.

## Overview

_Documentation coming soon._

This widget provides clock functionality to display current time.

## How to Add a Clock

1. Open your project in AVStudio and go to the `Editor` view.
2. From the elements panel under Widgets, select `clock`.
3. The clock widget will appear in your layout.
